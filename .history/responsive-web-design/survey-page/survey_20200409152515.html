Featured Researcher Survey

The External Media team would like to feature a profile of you and your research on the Bureau's home page. Please fill out the following 12-question survey for the profile. Your answers will be proofed and returned to you for comments before publishing.

1. Please give us an overview of your background (education, area of expertise, involvement in projects, etc.)

2. What are your current research activities?

3. What excites you the most about your current research?

4. What is the desired outcome of your research?

5. What do you need in order to make your research efforts more successful?
